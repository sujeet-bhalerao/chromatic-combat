Chromatic Combat

A graph coloring game

Rules

Two players, Maker and Breaker, take turns to color a graph's vertices. Maker's goal is to color the entire graph such that no two adjacent vertices share the same color. Breaker aims to prevent this. The game ends when all vertices are colored. If the coloring is proper, Maker wins; otherwise, Breaker wins.

Drag a color onto a vertex to color it. Good luck!
